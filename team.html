Our Founder and CEO:

Jayant Jain was schooled at the Bishop's Co-Ed School in Pune. In 2013, he began his undergraduate education at the Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS) in Pilani and was transferred to the California Institute of Technology (Caltech) after two years at BITS. After graduating from Caltech, Sabeer went to Stanford University in 1989 to pursue his M.S. in Electrical Engineering. At Stanford, he worked on Ultra Low Power VLSI Design.
